Research Engine · User Journey

From “I need to do research on X” to an insight a PM can act on Monday.

One continuous path. At each step you see what you do, what Research Engine does, and the checkpoint that keeps the work honest, either a judge pass or a guardrail.

Workflow step Judge checkpoint Guardrail

You open Claude Code and just say it

You

“I need to do user research on checkout drop-off.”

Engine

CLAUDE.md router recognizes the intent and starts the orchestrator. No forms, no setup.

1

Clarify the brief /research

You

Answer 5 to 7 sharp questions about the decision, the users, and the timeline.

Engine

Pressure-tests the vague brief, then scaffolds projects/checkout-dropoff/ from templates.

Guardrail · approval gate checks the source before any material is ingested
2

Plan the study research-plan

You

Cut 10+ candidate questions down to the vital few tied to the decision.

Engine

Builds in layers: questions → method → screener → discussion guide, auditing for leading language.

Judge · discussion-guide rubric: bias screen, methodological fit, decision linkage
3

Run & capture interviews research-interview

You

Moderate the sessions. The rapport and probing are yours alone.

Engine

Audits the guide beforehand, then cleans transcripts (“don’t summarize, just clean”).

Guardrail · PII → participants become P1..Pn, raw identities stay gitignored
4

Synthesize into themes research-synthesis

You

Own interpretation. Decide what the patterns actually mean.

Engine

Codes one transcript at a time, clusters into 6 to 10 themes with counts, quotes, and confidence.

Judge · synthesis rubric: quote fidelity, participant breadth, no false consensus
5

Communicate insights research-insights

You

Approve the headline and the two next steps you’ll actually pursue.

Engine

Writes 6-part insights, runs a skeptic’s pass, and format-shifts for Slack, repo, and leadership.

Judge · insights rubric: headline honesty, evidence proportionality, actionability
6

Share it on your terms

You

Confirm before anything leaves your machine.

Engine

Hands you ready-to-paste outputs at three depths. It never emails, uploads, or posts on its own.

Guardrail · no external sending without explicit confirmation

Total research time drops. Cognitive load shifts to judgment, the work that always mattered most.

## Warm-up (3 min)
- Walk me through the last online purchase you made. What were you trying to get done?

## Core — completing a purchase
- Think back to a recent checkout. Describe what happened, step by step, in your words.
  - Probe: What, if anything, made you pause?
  - Probe: How did you decide what to do next?
- Tell me about a time a checkout didn't go the way you expected.
  - Probe: What did you do then?

## Task (observed)
- Scenario: You want to buy this item and have it delivered this week. Show me how you'd do that.
  - Observe: where attention goes, hesitations, recovery from errors. Don't guide.

## Wrap-up (3 min)
- What did we not ask about that mattered to you?
- Anything you'd change about this experience?
